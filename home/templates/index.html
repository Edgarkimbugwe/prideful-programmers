Inclusive Spots

Inclusive Spots aims to be the leading global resource for the LGBTQIA+ community, providing trusted, up-to-date information on safe spaces where every individual can feel accepted, respected, and celebrated. We envision a world where LGBTQIA+ individuals can freely explore and engage with their communities without fear, knowing there is access to safe, inclusive, and supportive environments.
