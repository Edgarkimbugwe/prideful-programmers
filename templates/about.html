Who We Are

Inclusive Spots - is a dedicated app that curates a comprehensive list of safe spaces for the LGBTQIA+ community.

This platform provides easy access to LGBTQIA+ friendly cafes, clubs, bars, and support centers, ensuring that community members can find and enjoy welcoming and inclusive environments.

With detailed listings, interactive maps, and user reviews, Inclusive Spots creates a reliable resource that fosters connection, safety, and support for LGBTQIA+ individuals everywhere.

We aspire to be the leading global resource for the LGBTQIA+ community, offering trusted, up-to-date information on safe spaces where every individual can feel accepted, respected, and celebrated.

Inclusive Spots envisions a world where LGBTQIA+ individuals can freely explore and engage with their communities without fear, knowing they have access to safe, inclusive, and supportive environments.
